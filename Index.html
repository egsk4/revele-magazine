About Revele

Every image
holds a secret.

Revele is a visual arts magazine dedicated to the story that lives behind the photograph, the moment before the shutter, the world the photographer carried into the frame, the silence after.

We exist for photographers who understand that an image is never just an image. It is a decision, a feeling, a confession.

Each issue brings together submitted work from photographers worldwide, paired with the stories they choose to tell. Not captions. Stories.

What we believe
01
The gaze matters

Who holds the camera shapes what is seen. We celebrate the distinct perspective each photographer brings, without hierarchy or genre bias.

02
Story is everything

We ask not just for images but for the lives that produced them. Context transforms a photograph into something that endures beyond the moment of its making.

03
Intimacy over spectacle

Revele is drawn to the quiet, the overlooked, the tender. We have no interest in noise for its own sake. We are interested in what is true.

Submissions

Share your

We accept submissions from photographers at all stages. What we look for is not technical perfection, it is a point of view. Every submission is read personally by our editor.

Guidelines and requirements
Image specifications

Format: JPG or TIFF, sRGB colour profile
Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI
Dimensions: Min 2550 × 3300 px (8.5×11 in at 300 DPI)
Quantity: 6 to 20 images per submission

No watermarks. Submit the original uncropped frame. After your submission is accepted we will send you a file upload link.

Team credits — required

Every team member must be credited:

Photographer — Name @handle
Model(s) — Name @handle, agency if applicable
Stylist — Name @handle
Hair and Makeup — Name @handle
Creative Director — Name @handle
Wardrobe / Brands — label per image

Incomplete credits may delay or cancel publication.

The written story (optional)

You may include a written piece of 200 to 800 words — the story behind the images. This is what makes Revele different, but it is not required.

Exclusivity

Images must be unpublished and not posted on social media. After publication in Revele, exclusivity expires and you may share freely.

Response time

Every submission is reviewed personally. Expect a response within 7 to 10 days. All submissions receive a reply.

FAQ

Questions,
answered.

Do I need to be a professional photographer to submit?+

Not at all. We welcome photographers at every stage, from first-time submitters to seasoned professionals. What matters is your point of view, not your resume or equipment.

Can I submit work shot on a phone?+

Yes, as long as the files meet our minimum resolution requirements. Many phones can meet this. Check your export settings before submitting.

Is the written story required?+

No, it is optional. However, submissions that include a story are much more likely to be accepted. Even a few paragraphs can transform how we see your work.

Can I submit if I have already posted the images on Instagram?+

Unfortunately no. We require that submitted images are unpublished. After your work is published in Revele, you are free to share it anywhere.

How many images should I submit?+

Between 6 and 20 images. We recommend around 10 to 15 for a well-rounded series. The images should feel cohesive and tell a single visual story.

Will I be paid for my work?+

Revele is an independent publication in its launch phase. At this stage we do not offer monetary payment, but all featured photographers receive full credit, a digital tearsheet, and significant exposure across Instagram and MagCloud.

How long does it take to hear back?+

We respond to every submission within 7 to 10 days. You will receive a reply whether your work is accepted or not.

What genres or styles do you publish?+

All genres are welcome — portrait, documentary, street, fashion, fine art, landscape, conceptual. Visit our Mood page for the aesthetic direction of Issue 01, but do not let it limit you.

Do you accept series shot in colour and black and white?+

Yes. Both are welcome. What matters is that the choice feels intentional.

Can I submit to more than one issue at a time?+

Currently only Issue 01 is open. If your work is not selected for Issue 01, you are welcome to submit new or different work to future issues.

Issue theme — The First Gaze

"The story behind the image is the image."

Issue 01 of Révèle is built around the moment of first seeing — the first gaze between subject and lens, between photographer and light, between viewer and image. We invite work that captures not just what was seen, but the act of seeing itself.

There is no rigid brief. Interpret the theme freely. What matters is that the work carries a story only you could tell.

Featured photographers receive full credit in print and digital editions, a digital tearsheet, and promotion across all Révèle channels including Instagram. Révèle is an independent publication in its launch phase and does not currently offer monetary compensation.

Policy.

Last updated: June 2026  ·  GDPR-aligned · Doha, Qatar

1. Who We Are

Révèle Magazine is an independent visual arts publication operated by Ebrahim Kassem ("we", "us", "our"). Our website is revelemagazine.com and our contact email is inquiries@revelemagazine.com.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, how it is stored, and your rights over it.

2. Data We Collect

Submission data: When you submit work we collect your full name, email address, optional Instagram handle, series title, team credits, and optional written story. This data is stored securely in our database to facilitate our editorial process.

Newsletter data: When you subscribe we store your email address to send you editorial updates. Nothing else.

Technical data: Our server logs standard HTTP request data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and uptime monitoring. This is not used for profiling.

Cookies: We use essential cookies required for the submission system to function. With your consent, we may also use lightweight, privacy-respecting analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data
  • Submissions: To review your work, communicate editorial decisions, and if accepted, arrange publication.
  • Newsletter: To send issue announcements and open-call notifications.
  • Security: To detect and prevent abuse or spam submissions.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process limited data:

  • Render.com — our API server hosting provider. Server logs may be stored on their infrastructure.
  • Discord — we receive internal submission notifications via a private Discord webhook. No personal data is shared publicly; webhook messages are visible only to our editorial team.
  • Google Fonts — we load Cormorant Garamond and Inter fonts from Google's CDN. Google may log font requests per their privacy policy.
5. Data Retention

Submission data is retained for the duration of the editorial cycle plus up to 24 months, then deleted or anonymised. Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data Security

Submissions are stored in a secured database with access restricted to authorised editorial staff only, protected by bearer-token authentication. We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Passwords and credentials are never stored in plain text.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") of your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object to processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time for processing based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email inquiries@revelemagazine.com with the subject "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Essential cookies (always active): These are required for the submission and tracking systems to function. They do not track you for advertising.

Analytics cookies (optional, consent required): If you Accept All, we may use lightweight, anonymised analytics to understand page usage. You can withdraw this consent at any time by clearing your cookies or clicking "Essential only" in the cookie banner.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at inquiries@revelemagazine.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority if you believe we have handled your data unlawfully.
